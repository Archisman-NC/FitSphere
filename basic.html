How to Select Your Perfect Workout

Choosing the right workout is essential for achieving your fitness goals effectively and safely. Consider factors like your current fitness level, time availability, and specific goals, such as building muscle, losing fat, or improving endurance. Start with beginner routines if you're new, and progress to advanced workouts as your fitness improves. Remember, consistency and proper form are key to success.
