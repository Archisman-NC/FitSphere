3500 Calorie Veg Diet Plan

This 3500 calorie diet plan is designed for individuals looking to gain significant muscle mass and weight while following a vegetarian lifestyle. It provides high-quality plant-based protein and balanced macronutrients to support muscle growth.

Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Avocado Toast with Vegan Scrambled Eggs and Chickpeas - 800 Calories
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Vegan Protein Smoothie with Almond Milk, Peanut Butter, and Oats - 500 Calories
  • Lunch: Tofu Stir-fry with Brown Rice, Vegetables, and Cashews - 700 Calories
  • Afternoon Snack: Mixed Nuts, Dried Fruit, and Vegan Yogurt - 400 Calories
  • Dinner: Quinoa, Lentil, and Roasted Vegetable Salad with Tahini Dressing - 800 Calories
  • Evening Snack: Chia Pudding with Almond Butter and Berries - 300 Calories

Total Daily Calories: 3500

Additional Tips

  • Incorporate nutrient-dense foods like avocados, nuts, and seeds for healthy fats.
  • Consume a variety of legumes, tofu, and tempeh to meet your protein requirements.
  • Make sure to eat whole grains such as quinoa, oats, and brown rice to fuel muscle growth.
