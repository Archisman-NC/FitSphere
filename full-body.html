Overview

The Full-Body Workout is a great choice for intermediate lifters who want to target all muscle groups in one session. It focuses on compound movements that provide an efficient and effective full-body workout. This routine is excellent for overall strength, muscle building, and endurance.

Workout Schedule

This full-body workout can be performed three times a week with a rest day in between each session for recovery. Example schedule:

  • Day 1: Full-Body Workout
  • Day 2: Rest or Light Cardio
  • Day 3: Full-Body Workout
  • Day 4: Rest or Light Cardio
  • Day 5: Full-Body Workout

Detailed Exercises

Full-Body Workout Routine

  • Squats: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Barbell Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups (Assisted if needed): 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Overhead Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Barbell Rows: 3 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Dumbbell Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Tricep Dips (Bodyweight or Weighted): 3 sets to failure
  • Leg Press or Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Tips for Success

  • Warm up properly before starting each workout to prevent injuries.
  • Focus on maintaining good form throughout all exercises, especially for complex movements like deadlifts and squats.
  • Rest for 1-2 minutes between sets to allow adequate recovery and maximize strength output.
  • Progressively increase the weight as you get stronger to continue making gains.
