3500 Calorie Non-Veg Diet Plan

This non-vegetarian diet plan is tailored for individuals who want to gain weight and build significant muscle mass. It includes high-protein animal-based foods and healthy fats to support muscle recovery and growth.

Meal Plan

  • Breakfast: Scrambled Eggs with Spinach, Grilled Chicken, and Sweet Potato - 900 Calories
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Greek Yogurt with Honey, Walnuts, and Berries - 500 Calories
  • Lunch: Grilled Salmon with Quinoa and Roasted Vegetables - 800 Calories
  • Afternoon Snack: Peanut Butter and Banana on Whole Wheat Bread - 400 Calories
  • Dinner: Chicken Breast with Brown Rice, Broccoli, and Avocado - 700 Calories
  • Evening Snack: Hard-Boiled Eggs with Carrot and Celery Sticks - 200 Calories

Total Daily Calories: 3500

Additional Tips

  • Include lean meats like chicken, turkey, and fish for high-quality protein.
  • Incorporate healthy carbs from sources like sweet potatoes, quinoa, and rice to fuel your workouts.
  • Don’t forget to add healthy fats from avocado, nuts, and olive oil for muscle recovery.
