How to Choose Your Diet

Use the calorie calculator to determine your daily calorie needs based on your activity level and goals.

We provide both **veg** and **non-veg** diet options for different calorie requirements. Based on your goals, choose the calorie range that best suits you:

  • 2000 Calories - Perfect for weight loss and maintenance.
  • 2500 Calories - Ideal for maintaining muscle mass or moderate weight gain.
  • 3000 Calories - Best for building muscle or gaining weight.
  • 3500 Calories - Designed for intense weight gain or high muscle building.

Select the right diet plan according to your calorie needs and fitness goals.
